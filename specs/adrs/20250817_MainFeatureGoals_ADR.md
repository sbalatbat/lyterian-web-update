# Main Feature Goals

## Context and Problem Statement

*What do we want the website to be able to do and provide to users and devs?*

## Decision Drivers
- Need to consider what users need first and foremost when accessing the website
- Need to create structure for smooth development that fulfills our goals
- Focus on providing most necessary information

## Considered Options

- *Games Tab*: Show users the games that are in development / have been released and where to get them
- *Contact Form*: Allow users to contact the studio for questions, comments, and other messages
- *Map*: Allow users to locate the studio’s headquarters
- *Links*: Allow users to find relevant links such as social media
- Showcase achievements, accolades, press coverage, etc. to users
- Allow devs to provide information about themselves (contact, intro blurbs, names/aliases, etc)
- *Jobs*: Allow potential devs to find hiring/applying information easily

## Decision Outcome

- *Games Tab*: Show users the games that are in development / have been released and where to get them
  - Chosen as most important information to provide to users as a video game studio
- *Contact Form*: Allow users to contact the studio for questions, comments, and other messages
  - Chosen to facilitate communication between users and staff
- *Map*: Allow users to locate the studio’s headquarters
  - Chosen based on prior design as information to be provided, a minor thing to add
- *Links*: Allow users to find relevant links such as social media
  - Chosen for importance in maintaining an audience & retaining users
- Showcase achievements, accolades, press coverage, etc. to users
  - Chosen to highlight studio's successes and provide users with information on studio's reputation
- Allow devs to provide information about themselves (contact, intro blurbs, names/aliases, etc)
  - Chosen for devs to showcase themselves and their work on their own space

## Pros and Cons of the Options

### *Games Tab*
- Pro: The studio's main products are games, so having a dedicated tab for it makes it easier for users to see what we have.
- Con: Will need constant upkeep with production of more games.

### *Contact Form*
- Pro: We can set the parameters for users to contact the studio while keeping it smooth on the site.
- Con: Might need some external integrations for email communications in the backend.

### *Map*
- Pro: Relatively easy to implement and allows users to know where the studio is based.
- Con: Including the specific location of the studio may pose security risks.

### *Links*
- Pro: Allows users to connect with the studio and stay updated.
- Con: The studio may need to provide more frequent updates on the provided links for them to be useful.

### *Showcase Highlights*
- Pro: Promotes the studio's work and builds relationships with journalists.
- Con: Needs to be maintained to provide most recent and relevant highlights.

### *Dev Info*
- Pro: Allows devs to showcase their connection and their work to users and potential employers.
- Con: Usefulness depends on devs' desire to provide information and upkeep.

### *Jobs Tab*
- Pro: Keeps hiring information centralized and easy to access.
- Con: May take longer to implement, and can just be incorporated into the contact page.
